BlackPebble — Professional Solana Trading & Utility Platform

BlackPebble is a professional Solana trading and utility platform built for memecoin traders. It combines paper trading, market discovery, trader reputation, public profiles, trust scores, leaderboards, portfolio tracking, a recovery suite, wallet cleanup tools, and analytics into a single professional dashboard.

What BlackPebble is

BlackPebble helps traders discover opportunities, test strategies, build reputation, recover SOL, clean up wallets, track performance, and compare results through a transparent reputation network. Prices, market caps, liquidity, and volume are live and on-chain; paper-trading balances and trades are simulated, so you can practice risk-free. BlackPebble never takes custody of real funds in paper trading and never executes real swaps.

Who it is for

BlackPebble is built for Solana memecoin traders who want professional tools: people learning how memecoin markets move, traders testing strategies risk-free, competitors climbing leaderboards, and Solana users who need wallet utilities such as SOL recovery and account cleanup.

Live features

  • Paper Trading — buy and sell real, live Solana tokens with virtual SOL at on-chain prices. Risk-free trading practice with no real funds.
  • Market Discovery — browse trending Solana tokens with live price, market cap, liquidity, and volume. Token analytics, watchlists, and new token discovery.
  • Trader Reputation & Trust Scores — a transparent reputation network with trust scores derived from public, recorded trading activity.
  • Public Trader Profiles & Social Feed — public profiles, performance tracking, and an activity feed to follow and compare traders.
  • Leaderboards — trader rankings by realized P&L across daily, weekly, and all-time periods.

  • Wallet Cleanup Tools — review and close empty token accounts, surface dust and recoverable rent, and keep a Solana wallet tidy. Manual, user-initiated actions.
  • Trading Journal & Analytics — record trades and review performance over time.

Frequently asked questions

What is BlackPebble?

A professional Solana trading and utility platform for memecoin traders that combines paper trading, market discovery, trader reputation and trust scores, public profiles, leaderboards, portfolio tracking, SOL recovery, and wallet cleanup tools in one dashboard.

What is Solana paper trading?

Buying and selling real, live Solana tokens with virtual SOL instead of real money. Market data is live and on-chain; balances and trades are simulated, so practice is risk-free.

How does wallet recovery work?

SOL Recovery scans a connected wallet for unused or empty token accounts holding reclaimable rent SOL, then closes them on-chain to recover that SOL. It is separate from paper trading.

How do wallet cleanup tools work?

They help you review and close empty token accounts, surface dust and recoverable rent, and keep a Solana wallet tidy. Cleanup actions are manual and user-initiated.

What is a trader trust score?

A reputation signal derived from a trader's public, recorded activity, used to compare traders transparently through a reputation network.

What are trader leaderboards?

Rankings of traders by realized P&L across daily, weekly, and all-time periods.

Is BlackPebble free and does it use real money?

It is free. Paper trading uses virtual SOL and never risks real money. Only SOL Recovery touches a real wallet, on-chain and separate from paper trading.

BlackPebble is for entertainment and education only and is not financial advice. Paper-trading balances are simulated. Follow on X and Telegram.
