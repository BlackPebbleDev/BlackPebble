Zero-Fee Core Tools

BlackPebble is built around trust-first access. Core paper trading, wallet review, wallet cleanup, SOL recovery, burn tools, and account closing are zero-platform-fee tools. No custody, no seed phrases, no approval traps, and transparent previews before any wallet action.

Wallet safety

BlackPebble separates simulated trading, read-only wallet intelligence, and real wallet utility actions. Paper trading uses simulated funds and never executes real swaps. Read-only wallet analysis reads public blockchain data and cannot move funds. Wallet cleanup, SOL recovery, and token burning are real on-chain actions that you review and sign in your own wallet. Connecting a wallet is not the same as signing a transaction. BlackPebble takes no custody, never asks for seed phrases, and never asks for private keys. See the Wallet Safety guide for details.

Frequently asked questions

What is BlackPebble?

A zero-fee Solana memecoin trading intelligence hub for paper trading, perps, wallet review, public profiles, reputation, leaderboards, and wallet utilities, with community campaigns and Telegram community tools building toward launch.

What is Solana paper trading?

Buying and selling real, live Solana tokens with virtual funds instead of real money. Market data is live and on-chain; balances and trades are simulated, so practice is risk-free.

What are paper perps?

Practice long and short setups with simulated leverage, liquidation estimates, margin tracking, and perps P&L, without risking real funds.

What is wallet-linked trade review?

Connect a wallet in read-only mode so BlackPebble can review your past trades, hold time, token selection, sell timing, and risk behavior. No custody, no seed phrases, no approvals.

How do wallet cleanup and SOL recovery work?

Recover SOL from empty accounts, close unused token accounts, and burn junk assets. Actions are user-initiated and show transparent previews before anything happens on-chain.

Is BlackPebble free?

Core tools are zero-fee, including paper trading, wallet cleanup, and SOL recovery. Paper trading and perps use virtual funds and never risk real money; only wallet review and wallet utilities touch a real wallet, on-chain and without custody.

BlackPebble is for entertainment and education only and is not financial advice. Paper-trading balances are simulated. Follow BlackPebble on X and Telegram.
