====================================================
LIVE MARKET INDICATOR
====================================================

Add a visible live-status component near the "Trading Desk" title.

Placement:
- Directly beside Trading Desk heading
OR
- Directly below Trading Desk heading

Style:
- Small glowing green dot
- Text: LIVE
- Optional smaller text: Market Feed Active

Example:

● LIVE

or

● LIVE MARKET FEED

Behavior:

Green:
- Data feed healthy
- DexScreener connected
- PumpPortal connected
- Updates occurring normally

Yellow:
- Feed delayed
- Last update > 2 minutes

Red:
- Feed disconnected
- API unavailable
- WebSocket disconnected
