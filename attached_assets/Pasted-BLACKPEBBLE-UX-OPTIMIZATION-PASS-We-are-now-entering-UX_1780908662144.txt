BLACKPEBBLE UX OPTIMIZATION PASS

We are now entering UX polish mode. Do NOT add new features. Focus on simplifying the existing experience and reducing redundancy.

GOAL:
BlackPebble is a paper trading platform designed for both beginners and experienced traders. Every screen should be intuitive and self-explanatory.

==================================================

1. REMOVE TRADING DESK REDUNDANCY
    ==================================================

Current issue:

* Trading Desk shows trending tokens.
* Markets page also shows trending tokens.
* Markets page is objectively more useful because it contains:
    * Trending
    * Top Gainers
    * Volume
    * Migrated Tokens
    * Watchlist

Trading Desk and Markets now overlap heavily.

Evaluate the cleanest option:

OPTION A (Preferred):

* Remove Trading Desk completely.
* Rename Markets -> Trading Desk.
* Keep all existing Markets functionality.
* Make this the primary discovery page.

OPTION B:

* Keep Trading Desk.
* Remove all token lists from Trading Desk.
* Trading Desk becomes only:
    * Search
    * Current token trading panel
    * Positions
    * Active Orders

Choose whichever creates the cleanest user experience with the least redundancy.

==================================================
2. REMOVE HORIZONTAL CATEGORY SCROLL

Current issue:

Markets uses horizontal scrolling tabs:

Trending
Top Gainers
Volume
Migrated
Watchlist

This feels awkward on mobile.

Replace with a cleaner solution.

Examples:

* segmented control
* dropdown selector
* stacked category buttons
* another mobile-friendly layout

Goal:
Users should immediately understand how to switch categories without horizontal scrolling.

==================================================
3. REPLACE NEW LAUNCHES WITH MIGRATED

Do not show raw new launches.

New launches currently contain too much noise and display poorly.

Use:

Migrated Tokens

instead.

These are generally more useful and higher quality for traders.

==================================================
4. IMPROVE ACTIVE ORDERS SECTION

Current issue:

Orders are grouped by order type:

BUY LIMITS

WHOLESOME
WHOLESOME

TAKE PROFIT / STOP LOSS

WHOLESOME
WHOLESOME
SOLY

This makes scanning difficult.

Instead:

Group by TOKEN.

Example:

WHOLESOME

Buy Limit
Buy Limit
Take Profit
Stop Loss

SOLY

Take Profit

Much easier to understand.
