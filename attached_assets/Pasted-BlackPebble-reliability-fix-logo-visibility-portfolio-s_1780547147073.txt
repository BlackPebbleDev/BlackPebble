2. Portfolio stats must be accurate and consistent

Current problem:
Best Trade previously showed a winning trade, then later reset to 0.00 SOL even though closed trades exist.

This is unacceptable because leaderboard and future PnL cards depend on accurate stats.

Audit and fix portfolio stat calculations.

Definitions:

Starting Equity:
100 SOL unless stored otherwise for that user.

Current Equity:
Cash Balance + current value of all open positions.

Total PnL:
Current Equity - Starting Equity.

ROI:
Total PnL / Starting Equity * 100.

Total Trades:
Number of fully closed trades.
Do not count open buys as completed trades unless a position was closed.

Win Rate:
Winning closed trades / total closed trades * 100.

Best Trade:
Largest positive realized PnL from a closed trade.
If there are closed winning trades, this must never show 0.00 SOL.
If all closed trades are losses, show 0.00 SOL or “No winning trades yet”.

Realized PnL:
PnL from closed trades only.

Unrealized PnL:
PnL from open positions only.

Total PnL:
Realized PnL + Unrealized PnL.
